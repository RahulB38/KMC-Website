Recent Activities

Prayas, the college's PwD cell, supports differently-abled students academically and promotes their participation in extracurricular activities. It offers reading and writing services, organizes events like the Annual festival and Freshers' Talent show, and collaborates with organizations for workshops and training programs. Visually impaired students received scholarships from Help the Blind Foundation and the National Scholarship Portal.

Admission Process

1. Fill out the application form on the University website.

2. Pass theUniversity Entrance Test (CUET).

3. Qualify for the cutoff marks.

4. Undergo a counseling process.

5. Visit the college to verify documents and pay the required fee.
